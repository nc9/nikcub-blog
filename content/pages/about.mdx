Australian technologist and developer with a background in information security currently working as a freelance security and privacy consultant

Previously at [Techcrunch](http://www.techcrunch.com), [CrunchBase](http://www.crunchbase.com), founder at [Omnidrive](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Omnidrive) and a number of other startups since 2000.

Most recently at [CrunchFund](https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/crunchfund) in San Francisco where he sourced investments in [DigitalOcean](https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/digitalocean), [GitLab](https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/gitlab-com), [Streamable](https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/streamable), [ReadMe.io](https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/readme-io), [Pachyderm](https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/pachyderm#/entity) and [prediction.io](https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/predictionio#/entity).

University of Wollongong drop-out. Blogging since November ’99. Writer [at Techcrunch from 2007](http://techcrunch.com/author/nik-cubrilovic/). Named in Australia’s The Bulletin magazine [in 2007 as one of Australia’s ‘Smart 100’](http://wayback.archive.org/web/*/http://bulletin.ninemsn.com.au/article.aspx?id=266916).

Worked in the security field since the 90s publishing dozens of advisories around web application security, IIS, Windows NT, Linux etc. (under various aliases and [in my own name](http://0day.today/author/21985)). Today I am an advocate for user privacy, security, civil and online rights.
