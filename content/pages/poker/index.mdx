## Overview

Online poker tournament on **Friday the 6th of August 2021 starting at 6pm**

Two games:

 - Sit n go single table tournament at approx **7pm**
 - Ring game at the conclusion of the tournament

## Tournament Details

- Buyin - **$50**
- Max 10 players
- 20 minute blinds
- 100 big blind (BB) starting stack

## Tournament Links and Details

- [Zoom Meeting Link](https://us02web.zoom.us/j/87875923621?pwd=UHZHa1l0Q2dRTU9qakRXcjFubXh3UT09)
- [Poker Table Link](https://clubspoker.com/table-sessions/865d8992-6d1a-465b-ad7f-6967d74a7658)

## Payout structure

_Note: assuming 10 players_

- **1st Place** - $350
- **2nd Place** - $150

## Ring Game Details

 - Buyin - **$100**
 - Rebuy at any time in **$50** increments
 - Blinds **$1/$1** with no ante
 - Game begins at conclusion of tournament and runs until the last person folds
