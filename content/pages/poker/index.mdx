Online poker tournament on **Friday the 6th of August 2021 starting at 6pm**

## Details

- Buyin - **$50**
- Max 10 players
- 20 minute blinds
- 100 big blind (BB) starting stack

## Payout structure

_Note: assuming 10 players_

- **1st Place** - $350
- **2nd Place** - $150

## Required

1. Register for the tournament [by filling out the form here](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdQK5LLxb4xE2rutwVzsoa53tyHXEwu4qp-qikCWBwevEhNqA/viewform)
1. Signup for [Zoom](https://zoom.com) and install it on your laptop or phone - it is free and available for desktop and mobile. We will be using this for video chat during the game session.
1. Make sure you have PayID setup on your bank account so that you can load credit for the game. PayID allows you to make instant payments to another bank account. Your online banking should have instructions to register a PayID (usually a phone number). Here are the help pages for various banks:
   - [Commbank PayID](https://www.commbank.com.au/digital-banking/pay-id.html)
   - [ANZ PayID](https://www.anz.com.au/ways-to-bank/more/pay-id/)
   - [National Bank PayID](https://www.nab.com.au/personal/customer-support/payid)
   - [Westpac PayID](https://www.westpac.com.au/personal-banking/online-banking/making-the-most/new-payments-platform/)
1. The website hosting the games is https://clubspoker.com/ - You do not need to create an account and can play as a guest
